Using Navigation Tools
•
The breadcrumb trail of topics at the top of the page, for example, Working with protExpress > Managing Protocols > Adding a Protocol, shows the path you took to get to your current page. Click a breadcrumb link to go back to a previous page you’ve viewed.
•
Follow blue hypertext links or the Related Topics buttons on the help page to open closely related topics. You can also click at the top right of the help page to see the related topics.
•
To see definitions of terms used in protExpress, hover your mouse over maroon words with a dotted underline. A yellow popup window appears containing the word’s definition. You can also click these terms to open the glossary page.
•
To expand or narrow the width of the table of contents or topic pane, click and drag the divider between them.
