Using protExpress Online Help
To access online help while using protExpress, click on each web page to get help for the information or tasks on that page. See the following tips for locating topics of interest, navigating the help, and printing a topic.
Locating Topics of Interest
•
To view a complete table of contents, click at the top left of the help page. Click any topic in the table of contents to go to that page. Your current location is highlighted in the table of contents.
•
To highlight your current topic in the table of contents, click .
•
To view the help index, click the Index tab at the top of the table of contents pane.
•
To do a word search, click the Search tab at the top of the table of contents pane. Enter the query text in the search text box.
•
To create a list of favorite topics, navigate to the topic, click the Favorites tab at the top of the table of contents pane, and then click Add.
Using Navigation Tools
•
The breadcrumb trail of topics at the top of the page, for example, Managing Protocol Registrations > Selecting a Registration Coordinator, shows the path you took to get to your current page. Click a breadcrumb link to go back to a previous page you’ve viewed.
•
Follow blue hypertext links or the Related Topics buttons on the help page to open closely related topics. You can also click at the top right of the help page to see the related topics.
•
For definitions of terms used in CPAS-BioMart, hover your mouse over words that look like this: , to see a yellow popup window containing the word’s definition. You can also click these terms to open the glossary page.
•
To expand or narrow the width of the table of contents or topic pane, click and drag the divider between them.
Printing
•
Click at the top right of the help page to print your current topic.
