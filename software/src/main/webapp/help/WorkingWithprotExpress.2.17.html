Reviewing the Experiment
The last step in creating a new experiment is reviewing a summary of the experiment before finalizing it. While reviewing the experiment you have the opportunity to edit the experiment’s identifying information and its protocol details.
To review and finalize the experiment
1.
Arrive at the Review Experiment page. For more information, see Creating a New Experiment.
